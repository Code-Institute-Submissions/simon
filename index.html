Trivia

Get ready to watch, remember, repeat! The Simon game is the exciting memory game (very easy to pick up) of lights and sounds released by Milton Bradley in 1978 that became an instant hit world wide. Launched at Studio 54 in New York, players must repeat random sequences of lights by pressing the colored pads in the correct order. It's fast-paced play, with lights and sounds that can challenge you. Experience the fun as you repeat the patterns and advance to higher levels.

Hint

While the game is running, you may hold "Ctrl" to gain access to the hidden console.
